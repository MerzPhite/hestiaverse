<p class="text-muted mb-6">
  HESTIA VERSE is a resource for parents and educators who want to understand the online world young people live in. We focus on risks, patterns, and product features that can shape a child’s experience, so you can recognise what you are seeing and decide what is right for your family or school.
</p>
<div class="grid gap-4">
  <p class="text-muted">
    We do not tell you how to parent. We make you aware of what children and teens can be exposed to online, including the social dynamics, the content, and the incentives built into apps and platforms.
  </p>
  <p class="text-muted">
    You will find plain-English guides on common apps, trends, and online behaviours, plus practical pointers on what to look for, what language kids might use, and where to report serious concerns.
  </p>
  <p class="text-muted">
    Our aim is simple: clear, current, and usable information that helps you ask better questions and have calmer conversations.
  </p>
</div>
